SQL - Mellon City Mysteria!

SQL - Mellon City Mysteria is an exciting murder mystery game that my friend and I designed to transform our SQL practice into a thrilling interactive adventure. Inspired by the 'SQL Murder Mystery', we combined our skills in SQL and Python, utilizing the Streamlit framework to bring our vision to life.

Project Overview

SQL - Mellon City Mysteria is the brainchild of two graduate students from Carnegie Mellon University who love data and solving mysteries! üíª‚ù§

Here's how it all began: To improve our SQL skills, we discovered the SQL Murder Mystery, a fun way to learn SQL (check it out here). Inspired by the challenge, we wanted to make this game accessible to everyone, even those new to SQL.

Realizing there weren't many single-player murder mystery games online, we decided to combine our love for programming with our passion for mystery-solving. We took on the challenge of integrating two familiar programming languages - SQL and Python. We also used Streamlit, a tool for creating interactive apps in Python, to bring our game to life.

Our goal was to enhance the original SQL game by adding our own twists and creativity. This project allowed us to showcase not just our programming skills but also our creativity. We added interactive elements like secret codes and puzzles to make the game dynamic and engaging for players. We hope you enjoy it!
