About Me

Get to know me!

Hey there! I’m all about diving into the world of data with a big smile and making a positive difference wherever I can. With a Master’s in Data Analytics from the prestigious Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) and about five years of project management experience, I'm ready to tackle any data challenge that comes my way.

During my time in Singapore, I led a data collection initiative at the Ministry of Health that played a pivotal role in shaping policies for our aging population. Collaborating with diverse teams, we unearthed insights that sparked real change. Then, over at the Women's Center & Shelter in Pittsburgh, I took on the challenge of streamlining data processes, helping the company manage data more effectively and make better strategic decisions.

I’m deeply passionate about this field and am always eager to acquire new skills and grow. Feel free to reach out to me – I’d love to connect!
